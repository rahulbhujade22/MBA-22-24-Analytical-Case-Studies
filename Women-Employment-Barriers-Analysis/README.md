# 👩‍💼 Women Employment Barriers Analysis (Rural India)

## 🔍 Overview

This project analyzes the key barriers preventing women from participating in the workforce in rural India using survey data and data visualization tools.

## 🎯 Objective

To identify major challenges faced by rural women in employment and suggest actionable solutions for improving workforce participation.

## 📊 Data & Methodology

* Sample Size: 139 respondents
* Location: Vasai
* Sampling Method: Random Sampling
* Data Collection:

  * Surveys
  * Interviews
  * Questionnaires

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Power BI
* Survey Analysis

## 📈 Key Insights

* 62 respondents are employed, while 77 are unemployed
* 80 respondents have work experience, while 59 never worked

### 🚨 Top Barriers Identified

1. Gender discrimination
2. Family responsibilities
3. Lack of job opportunities

### ⚠️ Key Obstacles

* Lack of opportunities
* Family restrictions
* Timing issues

## 💡 Solutions Proposed

* Skill development & education programs
* Infrastructure development in rural areas
* Encouraging entrepreneurship
* Flexible work opportunities for women
* Access to financial resources and technology

## 📊 Deliverables

* Power BI Dashboard
* Survey Dataset
* Final Report (PDF)
* Presentation (PPT)

## 📌 Conclusion

The study highlights that women's employment in rural India remains low and requires combined efforts in policy, education, and infrastructure to improve participation.
